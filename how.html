How to Use

Follow these simple steps to record your screen instantly

Step 1: Open the Recorder

Go to our home page. Our client-side application will load instantly on any device without requiring any plugins or dynamic installations.

Step 2: Choose Your Settings

Select whether you want to record your Entire Screen, a specific Application Window, or just a single Browser Tab.

Step 3: Enable Audio Input

Check the audio toggle settings. You can capture system audio, microphone input, or both simultaneously for high-quality gameplays or meetings.

Step 4: Start Recording

Click the Start Recording button. A countdown or browser prompt will confirm that your stream capturing is currently active.

Step 5: Pause or Stop

Use the floating control dock to pause if needed. When finished, hit Stop Recording to automatically compile and finish the local rendering process.

Step 6: Preview & Download

Preview your compiled video in our local media player player instantly. Click the Download button to save the file in WebM/MP4 format with zero watermarks!
