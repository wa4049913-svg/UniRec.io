About Us

Welcome to Universal Screen Recorder Pro (unirecorder.online), your ultimate online utility designed for seamless, high-quality screen capturing directly inside your web browser. No complex installations, no sign-ups, and absolutely no watermarks on your hard work.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide a quick, lightweight, and 100% private solution for content creators, students, developers, and professionals who need to record their desktop or webcam instantly. We believe that professional tools shouldn't come with high costs or privacy risks.

100% Secure & Local

We respect your privacy. Unlike other platforms, our web tool operates entirely inside your local browser sandbox. Your recordings, system audio, and microphone feeds are processed in real-time locally on your device. We never upload your personal video content to any remote servers, ensuring full data confidentiality.

Key Features

  • ⚡ Ultra 2K Recording: Record up to 2560x1440 resolution.
  • 🎙️ Dual-Audio Support: Merge system/game sounds and microphone feeds effortlessly.
  • 🐢 Playback Controls: Export and test your videos with variable speeds.
  • 📱 PWA Optimized: Install as an app shortcut directly on your computer or mobile screen.

Developer Detail

This project is conceptualized, designed, and optimized by Waqar Ahmed. We continually work to update our capture engine to support modern browsers, delivering high frame-rates and stable WebM formats.
